OUR STORY

Meridian International Holding (MIH) headquartered in Hong Kong, a multi-national corporation, which has more than 30 years of operating experience in manufacturing textiles and garments, property development, management and investment.

Since inception, MIH had pride itself to strive to be the best in the industries it operates in. Our fundamental principle of operational transparency to our employees, shareholders, business partners and customers have won the loyalty and relationship of many over the years. The culture of integrity and passion can be felt in our professionals who are trained to endeavor for excellent and most importantly with customer-centre approach. Over the years, we have seen expansive growth through MIH’s diversification strategy and globalization efforts with office networks in Europe, Africa, China, Singapore and in particular, Cambodia.

In Cambodia, the Group has established itself as a reputable manufacture, exporter and employer of more than 1,000 employees across its business units.MIH’s confidence and commitment in the country saw more than USD 200 million invested in Cambodian projects that includes manufacturing of branded premium sweaters for its multinational clients and the developments of Industrial Parks, Residential and Commercial properties.


Our strict quality control and management of the building process entailed the adoption of MIH’s vertical integration system across our property operations – construction management, property management, asset management and hospitality services. Strategies for hospitality and resident services are an integral part of our product offering which includes partnership with established International award winning brands.


MIH is committed to a continual improvement of our products and services by leveraging on new technology platforms as well as the implementation of efficient and innovation solutions to stay ahead of the curve. Driven by its Motto “What is you see what you get” and powered by an experienced and dynamic team, the MIH brand is set to soar even higher in the years to come.
